Volunteer Bridge

A revolutionary new impact-driven social initiative


What is Volunteer Bridge?

The objective of the initiative is to act as an interface between volunteers willing to devote time and energy to social causes, and said initiatives that require volunteers. It aims to minimize the hesitations faced by volunteers, by providing them with a database of activities, classified by region, cause, and amount of time required.


They can choose activities in their neighbouring areas and take them up for the time period that suits them, thus eliminating issues of time, long-term commitment, and commutation.

Whereas at one point, finding time off my ridiculously busy work schedule for community service seemed impossible, with Volunteer Bridge, I can finally give back to society.
